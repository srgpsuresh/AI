NIN AI SaaS Platform

AI Research Lifecycle

Goal : Reduce duplicate research, improve knowledge discovery, identify research gaps and accelerate scientific publications.

Business Vision

Transform NIN's research repository into an AI-powered Research Intelligence Platform that helps researchers discover existing knowledge, avoid duplicate publications and recommend future research opportunities.

Current Research Lifecycle (As-Is)

Research Idea ↓ Search Google ↓ Search Internal Repository ↓ Download Papers ↓ Read Hundreds of Pages ↓ Compare Research ↓ Identify Research Gap ↓ Prepare Proposal ↓ Execute Research ↓ Publish

Business Problems

Problem Business Impact
Manual literature review Days or weeks spent reading papers
Duplicate research Repeated effort and funding
Knowledge scattered Difficult to discover previous work
Finding research gaps Mostly manual process
Proposal preparation Time consuming

AI Research Lifecycle (Future)

Research Idea ↓ AI Research Search ↓ Semantic Search ↓ Duplicate Detection ↓ Literature Summary ↓ Research Gap Analysis ↓ Proposal Assistant ↓ Research Execution ↓ Publication ↓ Knowledge Repository ↓ Future Research Recommendations

AI Services

Step 1 - Research Search Agent

Search all internal and external publications using semantic search.

Step 2 - Duplicate Detection Agent

Compare objectives, methodology and findings with existing publications.

Step 3 - Literature Review Agent

Generate executive summaries instead of reading hundreds of pages.

Step 4 - Research Gap Agent

Identify missing populations, unanswered questions and future opportunities.

Step 5 - Proposal Assistant

Generate objectives, literature review and draft proposal.

Step 6 - Publication Assistant

Suggest references, formatting and publication improvements.
